## Coastal Challenges to Address

- **Salt Crystallisation**: Causes blistering and peeling near balustrades and parapets.
- **UV Intensity**: Fades bright hues quickly between October and May.
- **High Humidity**: Encourages mould growth, especially in shaded courtyards.
- **Frequent Cleaning**: Rentals require rapid turnovers without odour.

## Calyco System Blueprint

1. **SaltGuard Metal & Masonry Primer**: Shields structural elements from chloride attack.
2. **CoastShield Elastomeric Topcoat**: Flexes with temperature swings and resists cracking.
3. **HeatGuard Reflect+**: Applied on terraces to reduce heat gain by up to 3°C.
4. **Aura Matte Interiors**: Low-VOC finish that keeps air fresh for guests.
5. **SilkGuard Satin Accents**: Washable for dining spaces and bars.

## Maintenance Rituals

- **Monthly Rinse**: Hose down walls to remove salt; follow up with soft brushing.
- **Seasonal Audit**: Inspect roof junctions and pergolas before monsoon. Log findings in [Calyco Tools](/tools).
- **Interior Refresh**: Schedule touch-ups during low tourist months; low-VOC systems dry fast, allowing quick rebooking.
