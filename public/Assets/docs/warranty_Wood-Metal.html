Wood & Metal (Enamels & Coatings) | Limited Warranty

Version 1.2 | Last updated: 26 Dec 2025

Thank you for choosing a Calyco coating system. This limited warranty describes what is covered, how long it lasts, conditions required, exclusions, and how to make a claim. Please read carefully and retain a copy with your proof of purchase.

Coverage

This warranty applies to Calyco products belonging to the Wood & Metal (Enamels & Coatings) category, including current and future finishes in this family (e.g., Dura-Shield Enamel, PU Wood Coating, Melamine Polish, Epoxy Paint, Anticorrosive Bitumastic Paint, Fire Retardant Paint). It covers film integrity (adhesion, no peeling/blistering), reasonable colour and sheen retention, and resistance to premature chalking/fading within normal residential/light-commercial use.

Warranty term: Up to 5 years from date of purchase when applied and maintained per Calyco instructions.

Conditions for warranty validity

  • Surfaces must be sound, clean, dry (<15% moisture for masonry), properly cured, and free from contaminants.
  • Follow Calyco surface preparation: remove loose/flaking material; treat mould/algae; repair cracks/defects; sand glossy areas; use recommended primer/putty where specified.
  • Apply within 10–40°C and RH < 80%, avoiding rain/condensation/direct sun on hot substrates during drying.
  • Use specified dilution, spreading rate, number of coats, and recoat intervals; stir during use and apply uniformly by brush/roller/spray.
  • Allow proper curing before cleaning; maintain ventilation and control moisture sources.
  • Retain invoice, batch details, substrate prep/application records, and dated photographs of the work.

Exclusions

  • Structural defects, movement, leaks, rising damp, hydrostatic pressure, efflorescence, or uncured/alkali surfaces.
  • Improper prep/application, over-thinning, contamination (oil/grease/dust/salts), painting on damp/uncured substrates, or non-recommended systems.
  • Damage from abrasion, impact, chemicals/solvents, fire, flood, vandalism, extreme weather, or atmospheric/industrial pollutants.
  • Normal ageing beyond warranty term; colour/sheens shifts due to application method, lighting, or tint variation.
  • Incidental or consequential losses, labour, access, tools, scaffolding, or accommodation costs.

Remedy

At Calyco’s option, the remedy is limited to replacement product for the affected area or refund of the purchase price of the defective product. Labour and incidental costs are not covered. Any replacement is covered only for the remaining original term.

How to make a claim

  1. Notify Calyco in writing within 30 days of discovering a defect.
  2. Provide invoice, product/batch details, surface prep and application records, photos, and site address.
  3. Allow inspection; if approved, Calyco will provide the remedy described above.
