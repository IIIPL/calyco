4FIRST AID MEASURES
Eye Contact:
Rinse cautiously with water for several minutes. Remove contact lenses if present. Continue rinsing. Seek medical attention if irritation persists.
Skin Contact:
Wash with soap and water. Remove contaminated clothing. Seek medical attention if irritation develops.
Inhalation:
Move to fresh air. Keep at rest in comfortable position. Seek medical advice if symptoms occur.
Ingestion:
Rinse mouth. Do NOT induce vomiting. Seek medical attention immediately.
5FIRE-FIGHTING MEASURES
Suitable Extinguishing Media:

Unsuitable Media:
None known
Special Hazards:
May release toxic fumes when heated
Protection for Firefighters:
Wear self-contained breathing apparatus and protective gear
6ACCIDENTAL RELEASE MEASURES
  • Eliminate all ignition sources
  • Ventilate area
  • Contain spill with inert absorbent material
  • Collect and place in suitable container for disposal
  • Clean contaminated area with water
  • Prevent entry into drains and waterways
7HANDLING & STORAGE
Handling:
Avoid contact with eyes and skin. Use in well-ventilated areas. Wear protective equipment.
Storage:
Store in cool, dry place (Cool, dry place (away from ignition sources)). Keep container tightly closed. Protect from freezing.
