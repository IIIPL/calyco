Acrylic Washable Distemper

Affordable washable finish for everyday living.

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Economical, water-based distemper providing smooth, matt interiors with good washability and quick drying—ideal for everyday living.


Acrylic Washable Distemper delivers a micro-smooth matt look with bright colours, fast drying, and good washability at an accessible price. Great for interior walls, ceilings, plaster, concrete, and false ceilings.

KEY FEATURES & BENEFITS
  • Smooth matt finish
  • Washable and fast drying
  • Low VOC
  • Economical interior solution

SURFACE PREPARATION
  • Surface must be clean, dry, and free from dust, grease, and loose paint
  • Remove all flaking or peeling paint mechanically
  • Fill cracks and holes with suitable filler and allow to dry completely
  • Prime new surfaces with suitable primer for best results
  • Sand glossy surfaces lightly to provide key for adhesion
  • Ensure surface is properly prepared for optimal results
APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Stir thoroughly before and during use to ensure uniform consistency
  2. Apply by brush, roller, or airless spray equipment
  3. For brush/roller application, thin with up to 10% clean water if needed
  4. Apply first coat evenly and allow to dry for 4-6 hours
  5. Apply second coat perpendicular to the first for best coverage
  6. Do not apply when temperature is below 10-40 C or above undefined
  7. Ensure adequate ventilation during application and drying
  8. Allow 7 days for full cure before washing or heavy use

⚠ IMPORTANT APPLICATION NOTES

• Coverage varies based on substrate porosity and texture
• Apply in thin, even coats for best finish quality
• Do not apply to damp surfaces or when rain is forecast

CLEANUP & DISPOSAL
  • Clean tools and equipment immediately after use with soap and water
  • Do not discharge wash water to drains or waterways
  • Dispose of wash water and empty containers according to local regulations
  • Do not allow product to freeze during storage
  • Keep containers tightly sealed when not in use
