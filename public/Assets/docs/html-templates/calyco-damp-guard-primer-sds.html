1. Identification

Product: Damp Guard Primer | Recommended use: As per product datasheet.

2. Hazard Identification

Classified as non-hazardous for typical architectural use. Avoid ingestion and prolonged skin/eye contact.

3. Composition/Information

Proprietary formulation. For solvent/PU systems, contains resin, additives, and appropriate solvents/hardeners as specified on the pack.

4. First Aid Measures

  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air; seek medical attention if symptoms persist.
  • Skin: Wash with soap and water.
  • Eyes: Rinse cautiously with water for several minutes.
  • Ingestion: Do not induce vomiting; seek immediate medical advice.

5. Fire Fighting Measures

Use foam, CO2, dry powder for solvent/PU types. Water spray for water-based types. Wear self-contained breathing apparatus.

6. Accidental Release Measures

Contain and collect spills with inert absorbent. Prevent entry into drains. Use PPE.

7. Handling and Storage

Store in a cool, dry, well-ventilated place away from heat/ignition. Keep container tightly closed. Follow label instructions.

8. Exposure Controls / PPE

Provide ventilation. Use gloves, eye protection; respirator when spraying solvent/PU products.

9. Physical and Chemical Properties

Appearance: As supplied. Odour: Characteristic. Flash point: N/A for water-based; see pack for solvent/PU. VOC: Refer pack/tds.

10. Stability and Reactivity

Stable under normal conditions. Avoid extreme heat and incompatible materials.

11. Toxicological Information

No significant hazards when used as directed. Avoid prolonged/repeated exposure.

12. Ecological Information

Do not release into drains or waterways. Follow local disposal norms.

13. Disposal Considerations

Dispose of contents/container in accordance with local regulations.
