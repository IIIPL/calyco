4 FIRST AID MEASURES

EYE CONTACT

Rinse cautiously with water for several minutes. Remove contact lenses if present and easy to do. Continue rinsing for at least 15 minutes. Seek medical attention if irritation persists.

SKIN CONTACT

Wash with soap and water. Remove contaminated clothing and launder before reuse. Seek medical attention if irritation develops or persists.

INHALATION

Move person to fresh air immediately. Keep at rest in a position comfortable for breathing. If experiencing respiratory symptoms or feeling unwell, seek medical attention.

INGESTION

Rinse mouth thoroughly. Do NOT induce vomiting unless directed by medical personnel. Never give anything by mouth to an unconscious person. Seek immediate medical attention.

Most Important Symptoms:

May cause eye and skin irritation. May cause respiratory irritation if mist/spray is inhaled.

6 ACCIDENTAL RELEASE MEASURES

Personal Precautions:

  • Wear appropriate personal protective equipment (gloves, goggles, protective clothing)
  • Ensure adequate ventilation
  • Avoid contact with eyes and skin
  • Keep unauthorized personnel away from spill area

Environmental Precautions:

  • Prevent entry into waterways, sewers, or confined spaces
  • Contain spill with inert absorbent material (sand, earth, vermiculite)
  • Do not allow to enter drains or water courses

Cleanup Methods:

  • Absorb with inert material and place in suitable container for disposal
  • Clean contaminated area with water and detergent
  • Dispose of waste according to local regulations
7 HANDLING & STORAGE

Safe Handling:

  • Use only with adequate ventilation
  • Avoid breathing mist or spray
  • Avoid contact with eyes, skin, and clothing
  • Wash hands thoroughly after handling
  • Do not eat, drink, or smoke when using this product

Storage Conditions:

  • Store in original tightly closed containers in cool, dry, well-ventilated area
  • Storage temperature: 10°C - 38°C
  • Protect from frost and direct sunlight
  • Keep container upright to prevent leakage
  • Keep away from food, drink, and animal feeding stuffs
  • Shelf life: 24 months in unopened container
8 EXPOSURE CONTROLS / PERSONAL PROTECTION

Engineering Controls:

  • Provide adequate ventilation (natural or mechanical)
  • Use local exhaust ventilation for spray application in enclosed spaces
