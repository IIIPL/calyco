Calyco Wood Primer

Safety Data Sheet | Issued: 30 Nov 2025

CALYCO PRODUCTS PRIVATE LIMITED, Plot 45, MIDC Hingna, Nagpur, MH 440016

1. Identification

Product: Calyco Wood Primer | Recommended use: As per product datasheet.

2. Hazard Identification

Classified based on product class. Avoid ingestion and prolonged skin/eye contact. For solvent systems, keep away from ignition sources.

3. Composition/Information

Proprietary formulation. See label for resin/solvent details for solvent/2K products.

4. First Aid Measures

  • Inhalation: Move to fresh air; seek medical attention if symptoms persist.
  • Skin: Wash with soap and water.
  • Eyes: Rinse cautiously with water for several minutes.
  • Ingestion: Do not induce vomiting; seek immediate medical advice.

5. Fire Fighting Measures

Use foam, CO2, dry powder for solvent/PU/alkyd types; water spray for water-based. Wear self-contained breathing apparatus.

6. Accidental Release Measures

Contain/absorb with inert material. Prevent entry to drains. Use PPE.

7. Handling and Storage

Store cool, dry, ventilated. Keep container closed. Keep away from heat/ignition for solvent systems.

8. Exposure Controls / PPE

Provide ventilation. Use gloves/eye protection; respirator when spraying solvent/2K products.

9. Physical and Chemical Properties

Appearance: As supplied. Odour: Characteristic. VOC/flash point: see pack/tds.

10. Stability and Reactivity

Stable under normal conditions. Avoid extreme heat and incompatible materials.

11. Toxicological Information

No significant hazards when used as directed. Avoid prolonged/repeated exposure.

12. Ecological Information

Do not discharge to drains/waterways.

13. Disposal Considerations

Dispose per local regulations. Do not pour into drains.

14. Transport Information

Check pack. Solvent/2K types may be classified as flammable/hazardous.

15. Regulatory Information

Complies with applicable coatings regulations in India.

16. Other Information

Information is provided in good faith; no warranty expressed or implied. Users must determine suitability.
