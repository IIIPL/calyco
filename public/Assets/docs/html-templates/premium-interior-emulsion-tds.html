Calyco Premium Interior Emulsion

Ultra Premium Interior Paint | Low Sheen Finish | Designer-Grade Color

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Calyco Premium Interior Emulsion is an ultra-premium water-based acrylic emulsion designed to deliver unparalleled color vibrancy and lasting beauty for discerning homeowners. This high-pigment formulation provides superior coverage with a sophisticated low sheen finish that is perfect for living spaces, bedrooms, and dining rooms.


Formulated with advanced acrylic resins and high-quality pigments, this premium paint offers excellent scrub resistance, burnish protection, and ultra-low VOC content for healthier indoor environments. The superior hiding power minimizes the number of coats needed while maintaining vibrant, lasting color depth.

KEY FEATURES & BENEFITS
  • High-Pigment Colour for vibrant, lasting results
  • Superior Coverage minimizes coats needed
  • Scrub-Resistant finish stands up to cleaning
  • Ultra-low VOC (< 35 g/L) for healthier indoor air
  • Burnish and scuff resistant for high-traffic areas
  • Designer-grade color accuracy and depth
  • Water-based for easy cleanup and low odor

RECOMMENDED APPLICATIONS
  • Interior walls and ceilings
  • Living rooms, bedrooms, dining rooms
  • Plaster and drywall surfaces
  • POP (Plaster of Paris) surfaces
  • Previously painted masonry
  • Wood and metal surfaces (with suitable primer)
SURFACE PREPARATION
  • Surface must be clean, dry, and free from dust, grease, and loose paint
  • Remove all flaking or peeling paint mechanically
  • Fill cracks and holes with suitable filler and allow to dry completely
  • Prime new surfaces with suitable primer for best results
  • Sand glossy surfaces lightly to provide key for adhesion
  • Ensure surface is properly prepared for optimal results
APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Stir thoroughly before and during use to ensure uniform consistency
  2. Apply by brush, roller, or airless spray equipment
  3. For brush/roller application, thin with up to 10% clean water if needed
  4. Apply first coat evenly and allow to dry for 2-4 hours
  5. Apply second coat perpendicular to the first for best coverage
  6. Do not apply when temperature is below 10°C or above 35°C
  7. Ensure adequate ventilation during application and drying
  8. Allow 7 days for full cure before washing or heavy use

⚠ IMPORTANT APPLICATION NOTES

• Coverage varies based on substrate porosity and texture
• Apply in thin, even coats for best finish quality
• Do not apply to damp surfaces or when rain is forecast

CLEANUP & DISPOSAL
  • Clean tools and equipment immediately after use with soap and water
  • Do not discharge wash water to drains or waterways
  • Dispose of wash water and empty containers according to local regulations
  • Do not allow product to freeze during storage
  • Keep containers tightly sealed when not in use
