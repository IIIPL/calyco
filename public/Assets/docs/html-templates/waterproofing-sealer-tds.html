Calyco Waterproofing Sealer

Flexible, Breathable Waterproofing Membrane | Elastomeric Crack-Bridging Protection

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Calyco Waterproofing Sealer is a premium water-based acrylic elastomeric coating designed to provide superior waterproofing protection for demanding interior and exterior surfaces. This flexible, breathable membrane locks out moisture while resisting UV degradation, fungal growth, and hairline cracking across concrete, masonry, metal, timber, tile, and asphalt substrates.


Formulated with advanced acrylic elastomer technology, this sealer bridges cracks up to 1 mm, adheres tenaciously to multiple surfaces, and maintains long-term weatherproof protection without trapping vapor. The breathable film mitigates blistering, while fungicides and UV blockers preserve integrity in exposed environments.

KEY FEATURES & BENEFITS
  • Crack-bridging elastomeric membrane (up to 1 mm)
  • Waterproof UV-stable protection for long-term exterior exposure
  • Vapour-permeable breathable film prevents trapped moisture blisters
  • Multi-surface adhesion: concrete, metal, wood, tile, asphalt
  • Low-VOC, low-odor water-based chemistry (below 45 g/L)
  • Excellent UV stability with accelerated weathering resistance
  • Resists fungal and algal growth due to in-film preservatives

RECOMMENDED APPLICATIONS
  • Concrete terraces and parapet walls
  • Exterior masonry, stucco, and plaster surfaces
  • Planter boxes and retaining walls
  • Wet areas, basements, and splash zones
  • Timber decks, fascia, and trims
  • Metal roofing and flashings (with suitable primer)
SURFACE PREPARATION
  • Surfaces must be clean, dry (less than 5% moisture content), structurally sound, and free from laitance, efflorescence, grease, and previous failing coatings
  • Remove loose material, laitance, and previous peeling coatings mechanically
  • Repair cracks wider than 1 mm with compatible elastomeric filler and allow curing before application
  • Treat cracks exceeding 1 mm with elastomeric crack paste and embed polyester tape over movement joints
  • Prime highly porous substrates with a diluted first coat (up to 5% water) or Calyco Acrylic Sealer
  • Mask adjacent surfaces and protect drainage points prior to application
APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Stir thoroughly before and during use to ensure uniform consistency
  2. Apply first coat using brush, 3/8" nap roller, or airless spray (0.021" tip, 2000 psi)
  3. Allow minimum 4-6 hours drying time between coats at 25°C and 60% RH
  4. Apply second coat perpendicular to the first for uniform film build
  5. For porous substrates, thin first coat up to 5% with clean water (brush/roller) or up to 10% for airless spray
  6. Reinforce joints, corners, and cracks with polyester mesh and sealant as required
  7. Do not apply when substrate temperature is below 15°C or ambient RH exceeds 80%
  8. Allow foot traffic after 24 hours; full waterproof cure achieved after 7 days

⚠ IMPORTANT APPLICATION NOTES

• Recommended film build: minimum 2 coats cross-rolled for uniform thickness
• Coverage varies based on substrate profile and porosity
• Do not apply if rain is forecast within 6 hours or substrate is damp

CLEANUP & DISPOSAL
  • Clean tools and equipment immediately after use with soap and water
  • Do not discharge wash water to drains or waterways
  • Dispose of wash water and empty containers in accordance with local environmental regulations
  • Do not allow product to freeze during storage
  • Keep containers tightly sealed when not in use
