Calyco Acrylic Wall Putty

Premium Acrylic Putty | Exceptionally Smooth Finish | Strong Foundation

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Calyco Acrylic Wall Putty is a premium acrylic-based wall putty that delivers an exceptionally smooth, white surface and strong foundation for paint. This professional-grade putty fills imperfections, cracks, and minor irregularities to create a flawless base for topcoats.


Formulated with high-quality acrylic polymers and fine fillers, this premium putty provides excellent adhesion, minimal shrinkage, and superior whiteness. The smooth, sandable finish ensures a perfect base for all types of paints while resisting cracking and peeling.

KEY FEATURES & BENEFITS
  • Exceptionally smooth finish for flawless walls
  • Superior whiteness enhances topcoat appearance
  • Excellent adhesion to various substrates
  • Minimal shrinkage and cracking
  • Easy to sand for perfect finish
  • Low VOC for safer application
  • Strong foundation for all paint types

RECOMMENDED APPLICATIONS
  • Interior and exterior walls
  • Plaster and cement surfaces
  • Concrete and masonry
  • Gypsum board and drywall
  • Previously painted surfaces
  • Ceilings and architectural elements
SURFACE PREPARATION
  • Surface must be clean, dry, and free from dust, grease, and loose paint
  • Remove all flaking or peeling paint mechanically
  • Fill cracks and holes with suitable filler and allow to dry completely
  • Prime new surfaces for best results
  • Sand glossy surfaces lightly to provide key for adhesion
  • Ensure surface is properly prepared for optimal results
APPLICATION INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Mix putty thoroughly to a smooth, lump-free consistency
  2. Apply by putty knife or trowel
  3. Apply first coat evenly, filling all imperfections
  4. Allow to dry for 4-6 hours, then sand smooth with fine sandpaper
  5. Apply second coat if needed for perfect smoothness
  6. Do not apply when temperature is below 10°C or above 35°C
  7. Ensure adequate ventilation during application and drying
  8. Prime surface before topcoat application

⚠ IMPORTANT APPLICATION NOTES

• Coverage varies based on substrate porosity and texture
• Apply in thin, even coats for best finish quality
• Do not apply to damp surfaces or when rain is forecast

CLEANUP & DISPOSAL
  • Clean tools and equipment immediately after use with soap and water
  • Do not discharge wash water to drains or waterways
  • Dispose of wash water and empty containers according to local regulations
  • Do not allow product to freeze during storage
  • Keep containers tightly sealed when not in use
