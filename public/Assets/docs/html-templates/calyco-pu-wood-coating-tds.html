Surface Preparation

Ensure surfaces are clean, dry, sound, and free from grease, dust, loose material, and contaminants. Cure new plaster for minimum 28 days (moisture < 15%). Remove mould/algae, sand glossy areas, repair cracks, and apply appropriate primer/putty as specified.

Application

  • Stir well before and during use.
  • Apply with brush/roller/spray as recommended; maintain spreading rate and coat schedule.
  • Avoid painting in direct sun/rain; apply between 10–40°C and RH < 80%.
  • Allow each coat to dry fully before recoating.

Safety & Handling

Use PPE (gloves/eyewear; respirator when spraying solvent/PU). Ensure ventilation. Refer to SDS for full safety information.
