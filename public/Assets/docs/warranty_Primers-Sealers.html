Primers & Sealers | Limited Warranty

Version 1.0 | Last updated: 30 Nov 2025

Thank you for choosing a Calyco coating system. This limited warranty explains coverage, term, conditions for service, exclusions, and how to make a claim. Please read carefully and retain a copy with your proof of purchase.

Products covered

  • Product: Interior Water-Based Primer
  • Product: Exterior Weather Primer
  • Product: Interior Solvent Primer
  • Product: Universal Primer
  • Product: Calyco Acrylic Wall Putty
  • Warranty period: Up to 5 years from purchase when applied and maintained per Calyco instructions.

Conditions for warranty validity

  • Surfaces must be sound, clean, dry (<15% moisture for masonry), and properly cured.
  • Follow Calyco surface prep and application guidance (primer/putty where recommended).
  • Apply between 10–40°C, RH < 80%, avoiding rain/condensation during drying.
  • Use recommended dilution, spreading rate, and number of coats; allow proper dry/recoat time.
  • Retain invoice, batch details, and application records/photos.

Exclusions

  • Structural defects, leaks, rising damp, efflorescence, or substrate movement.
  • Improper prep/application, over-thinning, contamination, or painting on damp/uncured surfaces.
  • Damage from abrasion, impact, chemical exposure, fire, flood, vandalism, or extreme conditions.
  • Normal ageing beyond warranty term; colour/sheen shifts due to lighting/application method.

Remedy

At Calyco’s option, we will provide replacement product for the affected area or refund the purchase price of the defective product. Labour, tools, and incidental costs are not covered. Remedy does not extend or renew the original term.

How to make a claim

  1. Notify Calyco within 30 days of noticing a defect.
  2. Share invoice, product/batch details, surface prep/application info, photos, and site address.
  3. Allow inspection; approved claims will receive the remedy above.
